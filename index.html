Instagram One-Way Followers Checker

Analyze your exported Instagram ZIP and find people you follow who do not follow back, plus pending follow requests.

Dependencies

  • Python 3.9+
  • beautifulsoup4
  • Tkinter (included with standard Python on most installs)
python -m pip install --user beautifulsoup4

Run

  1. Download your Instagram export ZIP.
  2. Start the app from terminal:
python main.py

The app opens a file picker so you can select your ZIP.

What You Get

  • People you follow who do not follow you back
  • Pending follow requests
  • Local processing only; your data stays on your computer
